About TravelPinHunt

TravelPinHunt is a community-built database of places where you can buy travel enamel pins around the world.

The idea came from a simple problem: when visiting a new city, it's hard to know where to find a quality pin to add to your collection. TravelPinHunt solves that by crowdsourcing pin locations from fellow collectors.

Each location tells you what types of pins are available — city pins, country pins, or place-specific pins — and links directly to Google Maps so you can find it easily.
