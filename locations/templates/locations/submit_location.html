Finding places to buy travel pins in a new city can be surprisingly hard — that's exactly why this database exists! If you know a spot where fellow collectors can find pins, we'd love to hear about it.

We review all submissions regularly and add verified locations to the database. To keep the quality high, we ask for a bit of proof — a photo of the pins sold there works best, or a detailed description of what's available. The more specific, the better!

A Google Maps link is required so others can actually find the place. Thanks for contributing — every submission makes pin hunting a little easier for every pin collecting enthusiast. 😌🙌
